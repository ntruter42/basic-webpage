Historical Places

Castle of Goodhope

● Built in 1666 by Dutch East India Company.
● Oldest surviving colonial building in South Africa.
● Served as military headquarters and prison.

City Hall

● Built in 1905.
● Houses the offices of Mayor and City Council.
● Site of Nelson Mandela's first public speech after release from prison.

District Six Museum

● Established in 1994.
● Commemorates Apartheid's forced removals, 1966-1982.
● Features personal stories, photographs, and exhibitions.
